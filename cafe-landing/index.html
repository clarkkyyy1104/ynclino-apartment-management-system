Our Story

A Coffee Shop Born from a Kitchen Table

Hearth Coffee started in 2019 when Mia and Joven Santos began roasting their own coffee in a small apartment in Quezon City. What began as weekend batches for friends and neighbors quickly became something bigger — a craving for community, for slow mornings, for a place that felt like home even outside of one.

Today, we source our beans directly from smallholder farmers in Benguet and Sagada, roast them weekly in small batches, and bake everything fresh each morning before we open the doors. Every cup tells a story — from the hillside farm straight to your hands.
